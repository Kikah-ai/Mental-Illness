Preventions:

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) sponsors the National Resource Center on ADHD The National Resource Center operates a call center with trained staff to answer questions about ADHD. The number is 1-800-233-4050.

Asking For Help:

- National Suicide Prevention Hotline: 1-800-273-TALK(8255)
- SAMHA Treatment Referral Hotline: 1-877-SAMHSA7(1-877-726-4727)
- Military OneSource(For Veterans): 1-800-342-9647
- DCOE Outreach Center(Specifically for PTSD): 1-866-966-1020
- Tricare: Health care program for veterans
- NAMI (National Alliance on Mental Illness): 800-950-6264 or Text 741741
